How we work

Production process

Clear milestones — you always know what happens next when working with our team.

1 Creative

Based on your brief, we propose strong creative directions within days — aligned with goals and constraints.

2 Script & storyboard

We develop the chosen route into a script and visual storyboard — budget and schedule included.

3 Pre-production

Call sheets, crew and locations, PPM, estimates — we plan the shoot down to the hour.

4 Production

On-set execution — gear, logistics, and quality for picture and sound.

5 Post-production

Edit, grade, sound, mastering, and technical review — delivery-ready masters.
